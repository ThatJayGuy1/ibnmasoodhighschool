Academic Excellence

At Ibun Masood High School, we are committed to providing a high quality and intellectually challenging educational environment that enables every student to reach their full potential.

Our Educational Philosophy

  • A belief that everybody has the potential to succeed
  • A commitment to instilling high aspirations and a desire to learn
  • A personalized approach to securing excellence
  • A passionate belief that each individual is unique and capable of rising above perceived limitations

Our Achievements

  • Steady growth in student population each year
  • Fully installed IT and science laboratories
  • Secured "O" and "A" level UNEB examination centers
  • Consistent improvement in national examination results
  • Theology students ranking second nationally in Sabia'a and Thalitha Idaad examinations for three consecutive years

Theology Program

Our unique theology program ranges from beginner to advanced levels:

  • Beginner Classes: Shuba Aalifu and Shuba Ba for introduction to Islamic studies
  • Intermediate Levels: Sabia'a and Thalitha Idaad
  • Advanced Level: Thanawi Class (senior theology class)

The school has acquired center numbers for national theology examinations at all levels.

Teaching Approach

Our teachers are trained to:

  • Set high expectations for all students
  • Create a sense of community and belonging in the classroom
  • Be warm, accessible, enthusiastic and caring
  • Inspire students with their passion for education
  • Collaborate with colleagues to improve teaching methods

Student Success Strategies

Strategy Description
Study Habits Create a good study space with little distraction, stick to a study schedule, do difficult homework when mentally fresh
Exam Techniques Prepare well to feel relaxed, read the whole test first, outline essay answers, answer known questions first
Time Management Have a personal timetable to track assignments and revision programs
Class Participation Ask questions when unsure, pay attention in class, take notes, concentrate on what is being said

Annual Science Fair

May 15, 2025 School Main Hall Grade 10-12 Students
×

Our annual science fair showcases the innovative projects developed by students across all science disciplines. This year's theme was "Sustainable Solutions for Tomorrow," with projects focusing on renewable energy, waste management, and eco-friendly technologies.

Students worked in teams for three months to research, design, and implement their projects. The fair was judged by a panel of local scientists and university professors, with prizes awarded for creativity, scientific rigor, and practical application.

Highlights included a solar-powered water purification system, a biodegradable plastic alternative made from agricultural waste, and an AI-based system for monitoring air quality in urban areas.

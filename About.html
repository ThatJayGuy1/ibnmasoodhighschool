About Our School

Ibun Masood High School is a private day and boarding "O" and "A" level secondary school situated on a 5-acre land in Kibiri, Busabala Road. It is a non-denominational Muslim-founded institute offering a dual curriculum—secular education for all students and theology (for Muslim students).

Our Mission

To offer a holistic education from within a caring and secure Islamic environment enriched with the values of discipline, tolerance, mutual care and respect, which extends beyond the school into the wider community.

Our Vision

Nurturing Today's Young People Into Well-rounded Citizens and Inspiring Tomorrow's Leaders.

Our History

The school was unveiled on 26th December 2006 and officially opened on 4th February 2007 with only 7 students. By the end of the first year, enrollment had grown to 71 students. The "A" level section was started in 2009.

The land was donated in the cause of Islam by the late Mr. Hamis Kyogoobe, whose dream was to see the land developed for two projects: a community-conscious school and a mosque.

From humble beginnings with makeshift classrooms, the school has grown to include fully equipped IT and science laboratories, secured UNEB examination centers, and continues to expand its facilities.
